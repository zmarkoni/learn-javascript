    Quick Overview
    SAMBO MRX SPIN Series reels are made to the highest specifications and closely compare to the Big Name Brands. This series sets a high standard with its spinning reel technology and a powerful multi-disk drag system. Beneath the spool there are Multi-Disc Oiled Felt Drag Washers which equalize drag force and dissipate heat, therefore providing a super smooth drag while under pressure. The Oscillation System offers excellent line lay while it maintains perfect stability. The reels feature a New Slimline Design with Machined Aluminium spools, Folding One Touch CNC Aluminium Sports Handles with Big Rubber Knob & 10 Stainless Steel Ball Bearings. Ideal for species such as Bream, Trout, Bass, this reel is sure to impress and perform.
